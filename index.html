Common Questions

Details customers usually want before choosing a dashboard, report, or app path.

Do users need to know code?

No. Users interact through forms, buttons, uploads, filters, dropdowns, dashboards, and exports.

Is this only dashboards?

No. Dashboards are the starting point, and ProgramMetrics can also support reports, trackers, portals, and lightweight business applications.

Can teams start basic and customize later?

Yes. Teams can start with Tier 1 dashboards, then upgrade to Tier 2 for guided customization or Tier 3 for enterprise applications, integrations, and systems.

Do users need Power BI or Python?

No. Customers work through uploads, forms, filters, dashboards, and downloads. Power BI or Python may be used only when a project specifically calls for it.
