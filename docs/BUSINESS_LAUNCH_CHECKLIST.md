# ProgramMetrics Business & Launch Checklist

This checklist tracks non-development tasks required to launch ProgramMetrics as a commercial software platform.

> Note: This is an internal planning checklist, not legal, tax, accounting, security, or compliance advice. Items that require professional review should be completed with qualified advisors.
