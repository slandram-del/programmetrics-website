# Industry Templates

## Purpose
Industry templates help ProgramMetrics choose better KPIs, visuals, reports, and narrative for common dataset types.

## Housing and Shelter
Common fields:
- client or youth identifier
- shelter or organization
- referral source
- denial reason
- admission date
- discharge date
- outcome

Recommended KPIs:
- referrals
- admissions
- denials
- missing demographic fields
- duplicate client risk

Recommended visuals:
- referral trend
- shelter comparison
- denial reason distribution
- outcome status
- missing field summary

Recommended reports:
- executive dashboard
- referral and denial report
- data quality appendix
