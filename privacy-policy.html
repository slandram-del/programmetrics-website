ProgramMetrics Policy

Privacy & Data Handling Policy

Effective Date: July 1, 2026

Overview

ProgramMetrics provides browser-based dashboard tools, downloadable templates, customized dashboards, and enterprise analytics services. This Policy explains how ProgramMetrics handles customer information and data files in connection with those products and services.

Tier 1 Studio File Processing

ProgramMetrics Studio is designed for self-service dashboard generation from standard Excel or CSV files. In the current Tier 1 Studio workflow, uploaded files are processed locally in the user's browser.

ProgramMetrics Studio does not intentionally upload, transmit, or store the contents of a user's Tier 1 Studio file on ProgramMetrics servers. Dashboard previews, charts, summaries, and downloads are generated in the browser session.

If the user closes or refreshes the browser session, the uploaded file and generated browser-session data are not retained by ProgramMetrics Studio.

Customer-Submitted Files for Services

If a customer separately emails, uploads, or otherwise sends files to ProgramMetrics for Tier 2 customization, Tier 3 enterprise work, support, troubleshooting, or project delivery, ProgramMetrics may access and use those files as needed to provide the requested service.

Retention, deletion, access, and confidentiality terms for custom or enterprise projects may be further defined in a proposal, quote, Statement of Work (SOW), or service agreement.

Customer Responsibilities

Customers are responsible for:

  • Providing accurate, complete, and lawful data.
  • Ensuring they have the right to upload, process, or send any files used with ProgramMetrics products.
  • Removing or de-identifying sensitive information before using self-service tools when appropriate.
  • Reviewing generated dashboards, reports, and outputs before relying on them.

Sensitive and Regulated Data

Customers should not send ProgramMetrics sensitive, regulated, confidential, or personally identifiable data unless they have permission to do so and the intended use has been agreed to in writing.

ProgramMetrics tools are intended to support analytics and reporting workflows, but customers remain responsible for determining whether their use of ProgramMetrics is appropriate for their privacy, security, regulatory, contractual, or organizational requirements.

Third-Party Services

ProgramMetrics may use third-party services for website hosting, payment processing, email, analytics, file delivery, or project communication. These services may process limited information necessary to provide their functions.

Tier 1 Studio may load browser libraries from third-party content delivery networks. Loading a software library is different from sending a customer file to that provider; ProgramMetrics Studio does not intentionally transmit uploaded Tier 1 file contents to those services.

Payment Information

ProgramMetrics does not intend to store full payment card information. Payment details should be handled by a payment processor or payment platform, subject to that provider's own privacy and security terms.

Website and Communication Data

ProgramMetrics may receive information that customers voluntarily provide, such as names, email addresses, organization information, project descriptions, and messages sent through email or contact links.

Website hosting providers and related services may collect standard technical information such as browser, device, IP address, referral, and access logs for security, performance, and operational purposes.

Data Security

ProgramMetrics uses reasonable administrative and technical practices appropriate for a small digital products and analytics services business. No website, browser tool, email system, or internet transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Data Deletion Requests

For Tier 1 Studio, ProgramMetrics does not intentionally retain uploaded browser-session files. For files or information separately sent to ProgramMetrics, customers may contact ProgramMetrics to request deletion or discuss project-specific retention needs.

Contact

Questions about this Policy or ProgramMetrics data handling practices can be sent to hello@programmetrics.io.
