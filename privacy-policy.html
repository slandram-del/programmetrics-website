Privacy & Security Rules

ProgramMetrics is designed to help customers upload and convert files, clean messy data, and create dashboard-ready reports without creating a long-term ProgramMetrics file store.

Privacy & Session Processing

  • Your uploaded file is processed only for your current browser session.
  • Files are not stored as customer projects or long-term ProgramMetrics data.
  • Previews can use your uploaded data with feature limits based on your Studio level.
  • Downloads, exports, and premium processing are available according to your unlocked Studio level.
  • Download any outputs before ending your session.

Payment and access rules

Payment checkout is handled by a payment provider such as Stripe. ProgramMetrics does not need to store card numbers to unlock Studio access. A completed checkout should return the customer to Studio with a paid access token or session that unlocks only the purchased tier and level.

Customers may preview higher tiers with uploaded data during the browser session, but locked previews are limited, watermarked, and may blur or hide premium sections. Downloads, full exports, reusable reports, workflow activation, and advanced analytics exports require the correct paid tier and level.

Customer data rules

Do not upload confidential, regulated, restricted, or sensitive personal information unless a separate written agreement allows it and the workflow has been configured for that data. Customers must only upload files they are authorized to use.

ProgramMetrics may use basic contact information supplied by a customer to respond to requests, support purchased access, and send service-related messages. ProgramMetrics does not sell customer contact information.

Third-party services

Third-party services, such as website translation tools, checkout providers, cloud hosting, or analytics providers, may have their own privacy practices. Customers should review those providers' policies when using those features.
